# Summary

Tightened customer-facing price policy and payment reminder scan bounds without enabling reminders or changing production config.

`salePrice` remains in `attributes.raw_source` but no longer becomes `Product.price` when catalog `price` is missing. Discovery no longer shows `0.00` or sends media captions with `0.00` for missing/invalid catalog prices; it marks price as requiring manager verification. Exact `get_stock` and `create_quotation` now fail closed for Treejar catalog items whose catalog `price` is missing, invalid, or non-positive; Zoho `rate` is not shown or used as a fallback in that case, the flow writes JSON-safe `catalog_price_fail_closed` metadata, and it creates a manager escalation. Payment reminder candidate scanning remains deterministic and paged, with a hard cap of `max(limit * 50, 500)` and a warning when the cap truncates the scan.

`docs/07-knowledge-base-spec.md` now states explicitly that `salePrice` is raw-only and not customer-facing until an approved sale policy exists.
