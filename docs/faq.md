## 1. Are you a manufacturer or trader?

We operate as a wholesale office furniture supplier with ready stock available in the UAE. In addition, we offer custom-made solutions through our verified manufacturing partners in China and Europe, ensuring quality control and competitive pricing.

## 2. Do you keep stock available?

Yes, we maintain ready stock of office furniture and office chairs in our Dubai warehouse for quick delivery. This allows us to serve urgent requirements efficiently.

## 3. Can you handle large office projects?

Absolutely. We have the capacity to manage large-scale corporate and government projects. Our international presence in Belarus, Estonia, Hong Kong, and Russia gives us strong supply chain capabilities.

## 4. Where are your products manufactured?

Our products are sourced from verified factories in China and selected European suppliers. Due to our long-term relationships, we ensure consistent quality and competitive pricing.

## 5. How do you ensure product quality?

We work only with verified factories and conduct strict quality checks before shipment. Our experienced purchasing team with over 20 years of industry experience carefully selects suppliers and materials.

## 6. Why are your prices competitive?

Because of our direct sourcing from manufacturers and long-standing supplier relationships, we eliminate unnecessary middle margins, allowing us to offer premium quality at wholesale prices.

## 7. Do you provide custom-made furniture?

Yes, we offer custom-made solutions based on client requirements including size, color, material, and design. We first understand your requirement carefully and then submit a detailed quotation.

## 8. Can we visit your showroom?

Yes, you are most welcome to visit our showroom in the UAE to experience the product quality personally. Our team will assist you in selecting the best solution for your workspace.

## 9. What is your delivery timeline?

For ready stock items, delivery is quick within a few days. For custom-made orders, delivery depends on design and quantity, but we always commit to clear timelines and execute professionally.

## 10. Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we provide professional delivery and installation services to ensure proper execution of the project.
